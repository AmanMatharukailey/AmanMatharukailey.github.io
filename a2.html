Shark


Sharks are a group of elasmobranch fish characterized
by a cartilaginous skeleton, five to seven gill slits on the sides of the head,
and pectoral fins that are not fused to the head.
Modern sharks are classified within the clade
Selachimorpha and are the sister group to the rays.
