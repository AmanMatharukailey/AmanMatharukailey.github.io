Lion


Lion is a social member of the cat family (Felidae).
It is the second largest cat after the tiger in the world.
Lions are carnivorous animals.
Their lifespan is about 10 to 14 years.
They live in large groups called prides, which consist of 15 to 30 lions including females (lionesses) and their young ones (cubs).
A lion's eyesight is five times better than that of humans.
