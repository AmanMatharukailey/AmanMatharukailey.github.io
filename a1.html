Starfish


Starfish (sea stars) are beautiful animals that can be a variety of colors,
shapes, and sizes, although all resemble a star.
While some appear smooth, they all have spines covering their upper surface
and a soft underside. If you gently turn over a live sea star,
you'll see its tube feet wiggling back at you.
These iconic marine animals are fascinating creatures.
